## 2. Project Overview
Following our meeting on [Meeting Date], **[Your Company Name]** will design, develop, and deploy a **web-based Accounting and Financial Management System** for the client’s Real Estate business operations in Bangladesh.

The system will manage all financial operations related to land projects—client installments, land purchase, salary expenses, refunds, cheque tracking, notifications, and bank/cash reconciliation.

**System Roles:**
- System Admin  
- Accounts Manager  
- HR Head / Head of Finance

## 3. Client Requirements (As Discussed)

| # | Requirement |
|---|--------------|
| 1 | Complete client & land management (RS Number-based) |
| 2 | Installment and payment tracking with receipts |
| 3 | Booking cancellation with refund and variable office charge |
| 4 | Expense and employee cost management |
| 5 | Multi-bank and cash-in-hand balance tracking |
| 6 | Cheque management with upcoming/due reminders |
| 7 | Automated and bulk SMS notifications |
| 8 | Approval workflows for all financial transactions |
| 9 | Comprehensive reports (Ledger, Trial Balance, P&L, etc.) |
| 10 | Data migration (2017–2025 historical records) |

## 4. Scope of Work (In-Scope)

| Phase | Activities | Deliverables |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Phase 1: Core Accounting Setup** | Land, client, RS Number tracking, installment schedule | Master data module, RS-wise balance view |
| **Phase 2: Collections & Receipts** | Booking, installment payments, receipts, approvals | Receipt workflow, auto SMS, reports |
| **Phase 3: Expenses & Payroll** | Expense categories, employee costs, commissions, refunds | Expense dashboard, refund workflow |
| **Phase 4: Cheques & Reports** | Cheque/PDC tracking, financial ledgers, dashboards | Cheque alert system, all standard reports |

## 5. Out of Scope
